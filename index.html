About Imtiyaz Civil Design

Excellence in surveying and civil design services

Imtiyaz Civil Design (TR0229128-W) is a professional consulting firm specializing in cadastral surveying, land development, and civil engineering services in Kuala Kangsar, Perak. We provide comprehensive solutions for land development, construction projects, and administrative requirements.

Our expertise includes construction-related surveys, engineering surveys, hydrographic surveys, global positioning systems, and advanced 3D land mapping with BIM consulting capabilities.
